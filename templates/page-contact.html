Frequently Asked

Are your courses fully accredited?

Yes. Every course on StudyMate Central carries independent accreditation from CPD UK, ATHE, ILM, or City & Guilds — recognised by employers across the UK.

How long do I have access after enrolling?

Lifetime access. Once you enrol, the course materials, videos, and downloads are yours forever, including any future updates.

Do you offer corporate group pricing?

Absolutely. We provide tailored group packages for 5+ employees including bulk discounts and a dedicated account manager. Contact us to discuss.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses. Contact us within 30 days of purchase and we'll issue a complete refund.

Can I study on my mobile device?

Yes. All courses are fully optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop. You can pick up exactly where you left off on any device.
